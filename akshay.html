Akshay Kumar



Akshay Kumar, born in 9 September 1967, is an Indian actor, producer and martial artist. 
He has been nominated for Filmfare Awards several times, winning it two times. He began 
his acting career in the 1990s. His appearances in films "Khiladi series", Waqt Hamara Hai 
(1993), Mohra (1994), Elaan (1994), Suhaag (1994), Sapoot (1996), Jaanwar (1999),etc is 
very famous.

His romantic films are Yeh Dillagi (1994), Dhadkan (2000), Andaaz (2003) and Namastey 
London (2007), drama films such as Waqt: The Race Against Time (2005), comedy films 
such as Hera Pheri (2000), Mujhse Shaadi Karogi (2004), Garam Masala (2005), Bhagam 
Bhag (2006), Bhool Bhulaiyaa (2007), and Singh Is Kinng (2008) met with acclaim. 
Housefull 2 (2012) and Rowdy Rathore (2012) with both of them grossing over ₹1 billion. 
His other films like OMG (2012), Special 26 (2013) and Holiday (2014) were highly 
successful critically and commercially. 

Apart from acting, Kumar has also worked as a stunt actor; he has often performed many 
dangerous stunts in his films like "Indian Jackie Chan". In 2008, he hosted the show Fear 
Factor – Khatron Ke Khiladi. In 2009, he was awarded the Padma Shri by the Government of 
India. In 2011 The Asian Awards honoured Kumar for his outstanding achievement in 
Cinema. He is a Master Chef too.
