Salman Khan



Salman Khan, born 27 December 1965, is an Indian film actor, producer and television 
personality. He is known as "The Tiger of Bollywood", "Blockbuster Khan", he is popularly 
called as "Bhaijaan" among his fans. 

His first film was Biwi Ho To Aisi (1988) then Maine Pyar Kiya (1989). Khan romantic film 
Hum Aapke Hain Koun..! (1994), the action film Karan Arjun (1995), the comedy Biwi No.1 
(1999), and the family drama Hum Saath-Saath Hain (1999). For his role in romantic drama-
Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1998), Khan was awarded the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor. 
Khan successful action films are Dabangg (2010), Bodyguard (2011), Ek Tha Tiger (2012), 
Kick (2014) and Bajrangi Bhaijaan (2015), Prem Ratan Dhan Payo (2015). Khan become only 
actor to give three back to back movies which collect more than ₹200 crore. The Government 
of India honoured him Rajiv Gandhi Award for Entertainment in 2008.

Khan is a stage performer and an active humanitarian through his charity organisation, 
Being Human.His tumultuous relationship with Aishwarya Rai, hunting of endangered species, 
and a negligent driving case in which he ran over five people with his car, killing one, 
have been extensively covered by the Indian media.[12] For the last of these, Khan was 
sentenced to a five-year imprisonment in 2015.
